Our service

We offer the following core services as a fiscal host, and we typically take a 10% overhead to cover our time on these services. We are registered in Germany (beginning in 2024) but able to work worldwide, subject to political context and availability.

Non-profit status

interalia is registered as a gGmbH in Germany, with 501(c)3 equivalency in the United States, which allows hosted projects to access funds from foundations and donations.

Human resources Management

We can offer employment to project members based in Germany, and we take care of contracting for project members based elsewhere. We offer a range of values-aligned HR policies to choose from based on your project and team.

Grant Management

We administer all grant agreements and support you with reporting.

Financial Accounting

We track and monitor revenues and expenditures, provide regular financial reporting and offer policies and practices to ensure projects have solid oversight over their financial health.

Risk and Compliance

We can help you understand what kinds of insurance make sense for different projects, and help you assess activities from a risk mitigation lens.

Hosting for Pooled Funds

In situations where various philanthropies want to work together on a pooled fund, we are able to host and redistribute funds.

In addition, upon request and subject to available funds, we can offer:

AD HOC Support for Individuals

For people facing administrative or bureaucratic hurdles, we aim to provide a friendly ear to talk through problems, whether or not you’re hosted with us.

Shared Tech Infrastructure

We can offer email, file storage, video conferencing software, and asynchronous chat software,, and website hosting, as well as a shared password manager and VPN.

Strategic advice

Depending on the topic or focus of your work, we might have the in-house experience to be able to talk through your strategy and approach.
